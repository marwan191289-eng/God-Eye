METHODOLOGY & LIMITATIONS

What this platform is: A real-time analytical tool that ingests live market data from Binance, computes 18+ technical indicators across 5 timeframes, classifies market regime, and produces probability-weighted signals with explicit risk parameters and invalidation levels.

What this platform is NOT: It is not financial advice. It does not predict the future. It does not guarantee profits. No technical analysis system can eliminate market risk.

How signals are scored: A multi-layer score is computed from six dimensions — Trend, Momentum, Volatility, Participation (volume/flow), Context (regime + sentiment), and Risk Quality. Each indicator contributes a weighted vote. Signals are filtered by market regime: in choppy or extreme-volatility conditions, the system can return NO TRADE rather than force a recommendation.

How confidence is reported: Confidence is derived from (1) absolute score magnitude relative to theoretical maximum, (2) multi-timeframe alignment, (3) data freshness, and (4) regime suitability. A 70% confidence does not mean a 70% win rate — it means the configuration of indicators, regime, and timeframe alignment is historically a higher-quality setup than a 40% confidence reading.

Risk parameters (ATR-based): Stops and targets use Average True Range (a volatility unit). Stop = 1× ATR; TP1/TP2/TP3 = 1.5×/2.5×/4× ATR. The recommended position size assumes you risk no more than 1% of your account per trade. You must adapt these to your own risk tolerance.

Known limitations:

  • Technical indicators are lagging by nature; they describe past structure, not future certainty.
  • News sentiment is keyword-based and may misclassify nuanced events.
  • Black-swan events (exchange failures, regulation, hacks, macro shocks) can invalidate any signal instantly.
  • Backtested performance does not guarantee future returns. We do not display a fabricated track record.
  • Data feed depends on Binance public APIs; outages or rate limits can degrade quality. The Data Quality indicator on the command bar reflects current freshness.

Your responsibility: Always verify signals against your own analysis. Use stop-losses. Size positions conservatively. Never risk capital you cannot afford to lose. Consider consulting a licensed financial advisor for personal investment decisions.
